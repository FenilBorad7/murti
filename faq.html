Why are your collections defined by year?

Our collections are curated according to the year of formation of the collection, usually one or two years after the works have been commissioned.

So collection 2017 will comprise works executed during 2015-2017. Collection 2016 will comprise works executed during 2014-2016 and so on.

How do you define most popular paintings?
Most popular paintings are the works that have been in the greatest of demand and have been placed in their new homes through auction. The works have gathered most of outstanding reviews from curators, collectors and clients.

ARTWORK

Certificate Of Authenticity

A Certificate of Authenticity is a signed document proving the authenticity of the work and containing details about the artwork for the collector's reference.

This document shall accompany the artwork.

The Certificate of Authenticity contains the following information:

1. Name of artwork
2. Medium
3. Dimensions of artwork
4. Edition
5. The date the artwork was purchased
6. Names of previous owners
7. The artist’s hand-written signature
8. Entity who authored and signed the certificate.

Frame

Proper framing is protection, preservation, and presentation to keep safe the investment/potential investment of artwork.

We use the best materials for the particular artwork. A framer’s work is just as much an art as the work of the artist themselves.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY

What type of packaging do you use?

Fine Art Shipping With Packaging & Crating Services.

Proven technique begins with a thorough assessment that considers factors such as product:

- fragility
- size
- value
- pickup conditions
- mode of transport
- environmental factors such as climate
- final destination requirements

Each requirement is factored into the appropriate container and cushioning system for ensuring damage free shipment.

About

Virtosu Art Gallery is the world’s leading online abstract art gallery, connecting people with collectible abstract art from the 21st century best artists.

Virtosu Art Gallery offers an unparalleled selection of collectible abstract paintings and, sculpture in a range of prices, and provides art lovers, collectors and best artists from around the world with an expertly curated environment.
