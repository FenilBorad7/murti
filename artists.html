Galleries looking for new artists are constantly reviewing submissions in order to add to their representation list. We are accepting applications online, see the form bellow. We are in search of artists with unique pictorial language.
Unique art derives from our visitors request for “strong and pure art”. Our artworks are not just mere visual representation of the world, our paintings are bearers of the spiritual dimensions and extraordinary experiences. We are welcoming the major representatives of the 21st century art movement worldwide, so we can establish a distinctly world style of painting.

"Our concern lies within the relationship between art and life. We care about explorations into unknown areas."
