Gheorghe Virtosu - Collection 2016 - Part 1


#12 "Happiness for a moment" - Solo Exhibition

Do you consider
yourself
adventurous?

ART COMPASS Issue 28

Artist Talk

Gheorghe Virtosu

What makes me happy? The concretization of a dream into the shape it has in my imagination. What makes me unhappy? When a dream cannot come to life. But I need to tell you something: the happiness we are talking about here does not last more than a moment because right away, another dream is born and I cannot relax until I see it materialized.
