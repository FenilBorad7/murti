"Political History" Solo Exhibition by Gheorghe Virtosu

25 July'18

"Political History"

Virtosu Art Gallery exhibition was key to establishing the pedigree for avant-garde abstraction proposed at the request of our visitors — a narrative that continues to shape the Gallery’s presentation of the abstract avant-garde. In the introduction to the exhibition, Alina Livneva declared that the day’s strongest artists had grown bored with usual painting facts. By a common and powerful drive, they were directed to abandon the imitation of objective natural appearance. To demonstrate the breadth of this avant-garde impulse toward intelligent art abstraction, our art gallery assembled a wide-ranging exhibition portal of nearly 700 oil paintings and sculptures. The exhibition portal is formed of thematic solo or group shows. An ordinary exhibition show usually has up to 10 artworks. Welcome to our exhibition page and enjoy the show.
